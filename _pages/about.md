I'm Luo Kehan 骆可瀚, you can call me Genghis. Now I am a junior undergraduate student in NYU Shanghai, majoring in Honors Maths and Data Science. I am the president of Math Society 2023-2024 as well as the Learning Assistant of Linear Algebra at 2023 fall semester. Academically, I have completed a [Dean Undergraduate Research Fund (DURF) project on Modern Optimization Theories](/https://genghis-l.github.io/projects/2024-MCM/), and [an individual research on Linear Algebra Theories presented at NYUSH Spring 2024 Academic Symposium](/https://genghis-l.github.io/projects/2024-MCM/). For competitions, I have attended 2023 Winter Mathematical Contest in Modeling and won Meritorious prize as a result. You can see my blogs, project details, CV, etc. on this portfolio website.
