## Overview

We initiate our project by studying fundamental Optimization tools, including First and Second Variation methods for single and multi-variable scenarios. We will progress to exploring Optimal Control Theory, focusing on Controllability, the Bang-bang Principle, Linear TimeOptimal Control, the Pontryagin Maximum Principle, and Dynamic Programming. We will adapt
real-life examples and formulate numerical solutions and visualization to them. Our final investigation will extend from Optimal Control Theory to its utilization in diverse fields such as Game Theory, Stochastic Calculus, and Partial Differential Equations.
